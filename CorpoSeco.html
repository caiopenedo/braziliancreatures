CORPO-SECO

(DRY BODY/UNDEAD)


A spirit that got rejected by heaven and hell for an unforgivable sin. After dead, the body gets rejected by the soil in disgust. Wanders during the night, like a corpse.

FEATURES

Body composed of skin and bones and long hair/nails


SINS

  • Tortured and murdered their own parents
  • Didn't keep their promise of religious pilgrimage
