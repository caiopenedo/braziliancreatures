BOTO COR-DE-ROSA

(PINK AMAZON RIVER DOLPHIN)


A species of pink dolphin in Amazon rivers. It can transform into a handsome womanizing man, seducing women to the bottom of the river and impregnating them.

FEATURES

  • Wear white clothes, shoes, and a hat
  • Transformation is not complete, the hat hides the dolphin's nostrils
  • Transformation at the full moon of June, during the festivities of Santo Antônio, São João and São Pedro
  • Turn back into dolphin at sunrise


    • BEHAVIOR

  • Takes the most beautiful woman at the party and/or virgin
  • Leave the woman pregnant and the child grows up fatherless


    • ORIGIN

    Justify a fatherless child and a virgin woman that got pregnant unexpectedly.
    Boto's child = every children who grows up fatherless
