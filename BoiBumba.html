BOI-BUMBÁ

(ORNATE OX)


A festival created in tribute to an ox that was miraculously cured by a shaman. It was injured for his tongue.


FEATURES

  • Dances, music, parades, and theatrical performance

  • Colorful allegorical festival

  • Characters dance while the story is narrated


    • MUSIC

    Guitar, cavaquinho, tambourine, rattle, triangle, and zabumba. “Toada” is the predominant rhythm.
