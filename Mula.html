MULA SEM-CABEÇA

(HEADLESS MULE)


A mule with flames for its head. It is a punishment for women who have intimate relationships with priests. It happens from Thursday to Friday, turning back at the cluck of a rooster.

BEHAVIOR

  • Terrifying whinny mistaken by a cry of pain
  • Attack whoever crosses its path at night (deadly)
  • Kick strong enough to seriously injure a person
  • Roam 7 cities by dawn


    • PREVENTION

    (from turning into the mule)
  • The woman has to be cursed by a priest 7 times before the start of a mass
  • Remove the frenum from the mule's mouth
  • Injure it with a sharp object

    • ORIGIN

    To reinforce moral standards, in the sense of making women scared so that they don't have sexual relations before marrying, especially with religious priests.
