CUCA

(ALLIGATOR/OLD HAG)


A hideous old witch/alligator who kidnaps disobedient children. She lives in a cave in the middle of the forest making potions and kidnapping disobedient children, especially those who go to bed late.

FEATURES

  • Often protrayed as an blonde alligator with long claws
  • White hair, wrinkled skin, skinny and hunchback
  • Creepy voice and keen hearing


    • BEHAVIOR

  • Solely motivated by evil
  • Lurks on houses' roofs, and abducts children after some mischief
  • Only sleeps 1 night every 7 years

    • LULLABY
