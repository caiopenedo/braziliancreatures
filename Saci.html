SACI-PERERÊ

(NAUGHTY PRANKSTER)


A mischievous dark-skinned boy who wears a red cap that grants him magical powers. He smokes a pipe and loves to play pranks on people and animals.


STRENGTH

Can disappear with whirlwinds he creates and is in every whirlwind that is formed.


WEAKNESS

Capturing: Throw a sieve in the whirlwind, remove his cap, and trap him in a bottle with a cross drawn on it.

When being pursued: Leave knotted ropes along the way to make him stop to untie.


PRANKS

HORSES

  • Suck their blood and frighten them at night

  • Make knots and braids in their manes

    • TRAVELERS

    Annoy them with high-pitched whistles

    HOUSES

  • Burn and sour cooking foods
  • Turn off the light of lamps
  • Disappear with objects
  • Swap salt for sugar
