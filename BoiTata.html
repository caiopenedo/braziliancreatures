BOITATÁ

(FIRE SNAKE)


A giant fire snake that protects forests, especially from arson. It has many eyes, from which flames come out, due to eating animals’ pupils. It lives at the bottom of rivers.


STRENGTH

Turn into a burning log and burn who set fire to forests
Its gaze can make a person blind, mad, or dead
Flame manipulation and night vision
Night vision


PREVENTION

Stand still and don't breathe

Keep your eyes closed


ETYMOLOGY

= “mbói-tatá”

TUPI-GUARANÍ | mbai = “thing” | tatá = “fire” | mbói = “snake”
